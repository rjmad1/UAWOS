# Universal AI Workforce Operating System (UAWOS)

# Federated Graph & Knowledge Architecture Standard (FGKAS)

## Version

1.0

## Status

Normative Standard

## Classification

Foundational Data and Intelligence Architecture Standard

---

# 1. Purpose

This standard defines the canonical graph architecture governing all data, knowledge, execution, governance, intelligence, and value realization within the Universal AI Workforce Operating System (UAWOS).

The platform SHALL operate as a federated graph architecture composed of independently governed yet semantically connected graphs.

---

# 2. Architectural Principle

No graph owns the enterprise.

All graphs collectively represent enterprise reality.

---

# 3. Graph Principles

## FGKAS-01 Federation

Graphs SHALL be federated.

---

## FGKAS-02 Independence

Graphs SHALL be independently deployable.

---

## FGKAS-03 Shared Ontology

Graphs SHALL share the canonical ontology.

---

## FGKAS-04 Unified Reasoning

Graphs SHALL support cross-graph reasoning.

---

## FGKAS-05 Explainability

All graph relationships SHALL be explainable.

---

## FGKAS-06 Traceability

All graph relationships SHALL support lineage.

---
