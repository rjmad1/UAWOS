## Version

1.0

## Status

Normative Standard

## Classification

Foundational Governance Operations Standard

---

# 1. Purpose

This standard defines the canonical framework for:

- Policy Management
- Risk Management
- Trust Management
- Compliance Management
- Exception Management
- Risk Acceptance
- Autonomy Governance
- Governance Enforcement

All governance decisions within UAWOS SHALL conform to this standard.
