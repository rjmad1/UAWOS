# Universal AI Workforce Operating System (UAWOS)

# Governance & Control Framework Standard (GCF)

## Version

1.0

## Status

Normative Standard

## Classification

Foundational Governance Authority

---

# 1. Purpose

This standard defines the governance system, control model, risk model, autonomy model, approval model, and compliance requirements for the Universal AI Workforce Operating System (UAWOS).

Governance is the supreme authority within the platform.

No execution, learning, planning, automation, recommendation, or optimization capability may operate outside governance controls.

---

# 2. Governance Mission

Ensure that all platform activity:

- Aligns to organizational intent
- Produces measurable value
- Operates within approved risk thresholds
- Complies with applicable policies
- Remains auditable
- Remains explainable
- Preserves human accountability

---

# 3. Governance Principles

## GP-01 Human Accountability

Humans remain accountable for:

- Strategy
- Governance
- Policy
- Risk Acceptance

---

## GP-02 Governance Supremacy

Governance supersedes:

- Agents
- Workflows
- Plans
- Learning
- Recommendations

---

## GP-03 Policy First

All execution must be policy compliant.

---

## GP-04 Explainability

All decisions require traceability.

---

## GP-05 Auditability

All actions must be auditable.

---

## GP-06 Least Privilege

All workforce entities receive minimum required permissions.

---

## GP-07 Continuous Oversight

Governance operates continuously.

# 5. Governance Authority Model

## Governance Board

Highest governance authority.

Composition:

- Human Decision Makers
- Governance Agents

---

## Governor Agents

Responsible for:

- Monitoring
- Enforcement
- Escalation
- Recommendations
