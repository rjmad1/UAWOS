# Preamble

The Universal AI Workforce Operating System (UAWOS) exists to transform objectives into measurable value realization through governed coordination of humans, agents, knowledge, resources, policies, and intelligence.

This Constitution establishes the immutable principles, architectural laws, governance hierarchy, and evolutionary boundaries that govern the platform.

All standards, architectures, implementations, packs, engines, graphs, agents, and integrations SHALL conform to this Constitution.

Where conflicts exist, this Constitution SHALL prevail.

---

# Article I — Mission

## Constitutional Mission

Transform any objective into measurable value through governed, explainable, continuously learning execution.

---

# Article II — Vision

## Constitutional Vision

Create a universal operating system for human and AI workforce coordination that can operate across any domain, industry, organization, or execution environment.

---

# Article III — Constitutional Principles

## Principle 1

Objective is the universal abstraction.

All execution SHALL originate from an Objective.

---

## Principle 2

Value Realization is the ultimate measure of success.

Outputs are insufficient.

Outcomes are insufficient.

Value realization is required.

---

## Principle 3

Governance supersedes execution.

No execution capability may bypass governance.

---

## Principle 4

Knowledge is an organizational asset.

Knowledge SHALL be preserved, governed, and continuously improved.

---

## Principle 5

Humans remain accountable.

Responsibility may be delegated.

Accountability may not.

---

## Principle 6

Trust is earned.

Trust SHALL be computed.

Trust SHALL NOT be manually assigned.

---

## Principle 7

Autonomy is earned.

Autonomy SHALL increase only through demonstrated trust and governance approval.

---

## Principle 8

Context precedes decision.

All decisions SHALL be context-aware.

---

## Principle 9

Evidence precedes recommendation.

Recommendations SHALL be evidence-based.

---

## Principle 10

Traceability is mandatory.

Every outcome SHALL trace to its originating objective.
